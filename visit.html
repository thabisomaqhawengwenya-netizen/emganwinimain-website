Plan Your Visit

Know what to expect before you arrive.

This page gives first-time visitors a calm, clear introduction to Sabbath worship, church rhythm, and practical next steps before coming to Emganwini Main Church.

What to Expect

A peaceful visit shaped by worship, Scripture, and welcome.

Friendly Welcome

Expect a warm greeting, a calm environment, and people ready to help you find your place.

Bible-Centered Worship

Music, prayer, Scripture, and a Christ-centered message guide the service each Sabbath.

Family-Friendly Church Life

Children, youth, adults, and guests all have a place to learn and grow within the church family.

Questions Are Welcome

If you are visiting for the first time, the church can help with seating, directions, and next steps.

Sabbath Flow

How the morning usually unfolds.

  • 08:30 AM

    Sabbath School

    Bible study classes for children, youth, and adults.

  • 10:30 AM

    Prayer and Fellowship

    A quiet transition into worship through prayer and connection.

  • 11:00 AM

    Divine Service

    Congregational worship, Scripture, music, and a Christ-centered sermon.
