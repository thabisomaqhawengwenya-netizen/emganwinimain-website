What We Believe

Faith shaped by Scripture, grace, and the blessed hope.

This page gives visitors a simple, respectful introduction to the beliefs that guide worship, discipleship, and church life.

Belief Summary
  • ScriptureThe Bible reveals God's character and guides life and worship.
  • SabbathThe seventh-day Sabbath is a gift of worship, rest, and delight.
  • HopeJesus saves, transforms, and will return again.

Bible-Centered Faith

We believe Scripture reveals God's character, shapes doctrine, and guides daily discipleship.

Sabbath Rest

The seventh-day Sabbath is a gift of worship, rest, trust, and joy in our Creator and Redeemer.

Christ Our Hope

Jesus saves, transforms, and will return again. This hope shapes courage, endurance, and peace.

Prayer and Worship

Prayer, reverent worship, and dependence on God are central to church life and spiritual growth.

Whole-Life Discipleship

Faith touches stewardship, health, service, relationships, and how believers live in the world.

Mission and Compassion

The church is called to share the gospel and serve people with practical compassion and love.
