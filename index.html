About Our Church

A welcoming fellowship rooted in Scripture, Sabbath, and service.

We are a Seventh-day Adventist congregation devoted to helping people know Jesus, experience the joy of worship, and live lives shaped by grace, truth, and compassion.

Our Mission

To share the everlasting gospel, nurture disciples of Christ, and serve our community with humility and love.

Our Vision

To be a spiritually vibrant church where families flourish, young people are valued, and every person is invited into God's purpose.

Our Story

Emganwini Main Church has become a place of prayer, Bible-centered worship, and neighborly care, welcoming both long-time members and first-time guests.

What We Believe

Faith shaped by Scripture, grace, and the blessed hope of Christ's return.

As Seventh-day Adventists, we seek to follow Jesus faithfully, honor the Bible, treasure the Sabbath, and share God's love through practical service.

Bible-Centered Faith

We believe Scripture reveals God's character and guides our worship, doctrine, and daily life.

Sabbath Rest

The seventh-day Sabbath is a gift of worship, rest, delight, and renewed trust in our Creator.

Christ Our Hope

Jesus saves, transforms, and will return again. This promise gives meaning, courage, and peace.

Whole-Life Discipleship

We value prayer, health, compassion, stewardship, and service as part of a life surrendered to God.
